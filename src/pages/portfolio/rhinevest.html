Simplifying Ideas

Making Financials Easy

The goal of RhineVest is to make financials and investing as straightforward and user friendly as possible but combining technology with real people. The sites design and messaging really focused around making sure the end user understood the concepts provided. Simple Ideas. Simple Graphics. Simple Finances.

Keeping Users Safe

Adding SSL Support

Due to RhineVest's financial nature there are some web standards that must be followed. The major being that the site needed to be secure. RhineVest is a startup and wanted to achieve all of their web management needs cheaply but effectively. So, the site is hosted for free on Github, the domain was bought from Google Domains, and the ssl and DNS was free from CloudFlare. Meaning that web admin costs are about $12/yr. You can read more about how this process works on the Di blog.
